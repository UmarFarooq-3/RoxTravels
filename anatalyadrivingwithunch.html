Antalya diving tour with lunch and transfer

This unique experience begins when you are picked up from your hotel by our team members. Before your dive, a briefing will take place with our professional scuba dive instructors providing important safety and technical information. The full-day scuba diving adventure includes a boat trip and 2 dives suitable for both beginners and experienced divers. Each dive has a maximum duration of 20 minutes for safety reasons. Your boat's destination will be the area of Kemer in the Antalya region.

Pickup:

The Antalya Diving excursion starts with your pickup from the hotel. Our team members will drive you to the port where you will meet your scuba dive instructors.

Diving Boat:

The tour includes two dives on the same day. Once at the port, our spacious and fully equipped diving boat will be waiting for you. It features sunbeds, toilets, showers, and a bar offering coffee and traditional Turkish tea.

Instructions:

Before your dive, a briefing will take place aboard. Professional scuba dive instructors will provide important safety and technical information, demonstrate proper equipment usage, and conduct tests with professional oxygen tubes. They will also demonstrate underwater communication using sign language, ensuring a clear understanding, especially for first-time divers.

1st Dive:

The first dive takes place in the area of Kemer, known for its warm and clear waters, providing maximum visibility. Diving spots are randomly chosen by instructors on the same day.

Lunch:

After the first dive, a lunch break follows aboard, offering a nutritious meal to energize and support you for the next dive. Turkish tea or coffee is also available.

2nd Dive:

After resting and refueling, the boat takes you for the second dive at a different spot, providing an opportunity to discover something new and compare the spots.

Photos & Videos:

Official photos and videos will be taken during your dives, available for preview and purchase at an extra cost once you reach the port, serving as great souvenirs.

Return Back:

The Antalya Diving excursion concludes upon reaching the port. Full of emotions and a pleasant tiredness, you will be transferred back to your hotel.

  • Diving Equipments
  • Training Service
  • Full Insurance
  • 2 Dives
  • Lunch

What's Excluded
