Bodrum Kos Island Tour

If you want to fit two beautiful countries into one amazing vacation, our Kos Island Tour from Bodrum is exactly what you need. Some of the Greek islands are so close to Turkish coastline, that it looks as if you can take a short swim and clime their shore. We’ve decided to make that dream a reality and we are taking you to visit the gorgeous Kos Island directly from Bodrum. Our fantastic journey starts with a free pick-up from your hotel. Our driver will take you for a short and comfy ride to Bodrum port, where you will board our impressive high-speed ferry, and we will sail west, toward the gorgeous Kos Island.

After a smooth twenty-minute ferry journey across the Aegean, we will step onto Greek soil. In the harbor of Kos Town. You will have eight hours of free time to roam its charming narrow streets. With its unique scenery of beautifully pastel-colored houses, archeological sites and lovely churches, Kos is a new dimension on its own. With numerous temples, castles, theatres, as well its modern town square vibrant with chic cafes and stylish shops, Kos Island is every tourist’s dream.

Kos is a second largest of the Dodecanese Islands, located 12 miles west from Bodrum. This wonderful island has its place in history as well. It is a birthplace of Hippocrates, the father of medicine. You will get a chance to take a walk to the Hippocrates tree, under which, according to the legend, Hippocrates taught his pupils the art of medicine. You can also visit the archeological site of Asklepieion. Asklepieion the oldest healing center of the ancient world and the most outstanding monument of the Kos Island. After those delightful eight hours of peaceful and fascinating sightseeing are over, you will meet the rest of your group in the harbor and sail back to Bodrum. Upon your arrival, our driver will pick you up and take you back to your hotel.

Don’t miss this wonderful opportunity to see this captivating and unique island without the fuss of the usual tourist queues. Take a gentle wander through history, at your own pace. Let the miracles of the old days tell their story, as you feast your eyes on the beauties of this exquisite island.
