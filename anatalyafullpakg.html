Antalya Pamukkale Full Package (Tickets & Lunch)

The day trip kicks off in the early morning, with our comfortable vehicle picking you up at your pre-arranged hotel time in Antalya. This guided full-day tour to Pamukkale ensures you have a professional and experienced tour guide to enhance your exploration.

Pick Up:

Departure from Antalya occurs in the early morning, with a fully air-conditioned bus providing a comfortable ride from your hotel. Our tour guide will greet you, providing final details about the day's schedule.

Comfort Stops:

Given the distance from Antalya to Pamukkale, the journey includes several short stops for your comfort. Enjoy an energizing breakfast at Korkuteli and take in the panoramic views over Pamukkale during a break at Denizli, where lunch is served at a local restaurant.

Hierapolis Ancient City:

Explore the UNESCO-listed ancient city of Hierapolis, featuring a theatre, necropolis, temples, and Roman baths. This spiritual and healing center from antiquity adds a rich historical dimension to your day.

Cotton Castle:

Visit the stunning Pamukkale Travertines, also known as Cotton Castle. This natural wonder boasts white-washed terraces formed by thermal water, offering a captivating and picturesque landscape for exploration and photography.

Cleopatra’s Pool:

Enjoy a visit to Cleopatra’s Pool, an antique thermal pool known for its beneficial effects on muscles and bones. Entrance to the pool is available at an extra cost for those wishing to take a dip.

Return:

After a rejuvenating day, board the bus and return to Antalya, concluding the excursion in the evening when you reach your hotel.

Hotel Pick-up Note:

For guests staying in small hotels in Antalya's old town region (KALEICI HOTELS), please meet us in front of McDonald's as buses cannot enter narrow streets.

Don't Forget
  • 2-6 age group guests can join with a 50% discount; entrance fee is extra for guests older than 6 years.
  • Children need valid passports for museum entrance validation.
  • Bring towels, swimming costumes, sun cream, sunglasses, camera, comfortable clothing, comfortable shoes, and a hat.
  • Consider snacks for a long day, especially for guests with walking difficulties.
  • Receive the exact pick-up time from our operation team a day before the tour.
