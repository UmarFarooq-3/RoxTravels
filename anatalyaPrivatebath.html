Full Private Turkish Bath

Treat yourself with an extraordinary and memorable day at Peloid by joining the Full Private Turkish Bath experience during your holidays in Antalya. Peloid stands out as one of the most unique spa-like facilities in the country, offering not only an authentic Turkish Bath but also a variety of treatments designed to restore balance to your soul and body. With multiple buildings, rooms, and outdoor treatment areas, Peloid promises to provide you with a special and rejuvenating time like no other.

The Full Private Turkish Bath takes place in a private VIP villa specially designed to meet even the most demanding expectations. Transfers to Peloid are included in the price for your convenience. Once there, prepare to be impressed by the VIP suite, equipped with modern amenities for a pleasant and smooth stay.

When it comes to the treatment, your experience begins with a session in the sauna room. The high temperature and steams help your body release toxins, focusing on preparing your body and mind to relax.

Afterwards, you'll enter the salt room to enjoy the therapeutic benefits of salt minerals. A professional will offer you a full-body scrub for 20 minutes, essential for your skin's health as it removes dead cells. Following the scrub, enjoy a foam massage and body wash, leaving your skin smooth, soft, and revitalized.

The VIP package aims to balance your mind and body with two massage sessions: a 30-minute full-body massage and a 20-minute foot massage. Specifically, you'll indulge in a relaxing Bali massage in your private villa.

For the final part of this rejuvenating experience, enter the outdoor mud pool and spend some time there. Mud is renowned for its therapeutic benefits for the skin and muscles. If you wish to cool down and relax further, take a swim in the facility's pool. Our highly experienced staff will offer you a cup of fragrant Turkish tea, famous for its relaxing effects.

The Full Private Turkish Bath in Antalya is one of the most exclusive activities to spoil yourself. Escape the crowds, discover the secrets of the authentic Turkish bath, and treat yourself to a special and memorable experience.
