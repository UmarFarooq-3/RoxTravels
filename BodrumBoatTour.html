Bodrum Turkish Bath

Join an exquisite and ultra-relaxing cruise into the blue waters of the Aegean Sea. This full-day Bodrum Boat Trip ensures a day full of laughter, joy, and pleasurable moments. Visit secluded bays, unspoiled islands, swim in the refreshing waters of the Aegean, and admire breath-taking views.

Tour Details
Duration: 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
Pick Up:

During the morning time, a fully air-conditioned vehicle will pick you up from your hotel in Bodrum.

The Boat:

Travel on a spacious, double-deck boat equipped with a comfortable front deck for sun-seekers.

Introductory Briefing
Aquarium Bay:

Crystal clear water gives the feeling of swimming in an aquarium.

Black Island:

Known for thermal springs with minerals beneficial for skin and muscle pains (extra cost for entering springs).

Meteor Pit: Created by a falling meteor, one of the most attractive places with clear water at a depth of 20 meters.

Lunch:

Enjoy a delicious and freshly made lunch on the boat.

Rabbit Island:

An amazing swimming stop in a beautiful, unspoiled bay with a chance to observe rabbits.

Camel Beach:

The last stop with an option for an ultra-exciting camel ride (extra cost).

Return:

The cruise finishes during the afternoon, and a comfortable car will drive you back to your accommodation before sunset.

What's Included

  • Full Insurance
  • Lunch

What's Excluded

  • Personal Expenditure
  • Other Drinks
  • Snacks
Don't Forget

Remember to protect your electronic equipment from dust and water. Bring towels, comfortable clothing, shoes, swimming costumes, sun cream, sunglasses, camera, and a hat.

Free transfer service is available for Bodrum Boat Trip from Gumbet, Bitez, Ortakent, and Bodrum hotels. If your hotel is in another location, transfer is available with a fee.

Please note the three different packages: "Soft Drinks," "All Inclusive," or "Foam Party" boats. Book the same package with your company to avoid separation.
