Bodrum Rhodes Day trip

Join an amazing daily Bodrum Rhodes Day Trip to the Greek Island of Rhodes and explore its history, relax on the beach and feel its incredible atmosphere. Add an extra touch of excitement during your holidays by enjoying a boat trip into the Aegean Sea and by visiting a Greek island for a day. This memorable activity is the perfect option for those seeking some more adventure, solo travelers, families, or groups of friends.

During holidays in Bodrum, you might be interested in exploring some nearby Greek Islands located in the Dodecanese Island complex. Bodrum’s close proximity to these islands allows visitors to upgrade their vacation experience with a daily trip there. Rhodes is the largest and most cosmopolitan island of the Dodecanese. Thus, do not miss the chance to explore it and feel its vibes. Thus, Bodrum Rhodes Day Trip is ideal for all; solo travelers, groups of youngsters, and families.

Hotel Pick-up: 08:30AM

Tour Times: 08:00 / 18:00

Boat Trip to Rhodes from Bodrum:

The daily excursion to the island of Rhodes begins during the morning. Participants will meet at the port from where the boat departs. After completing all the necessary formalities, you will enter the ferry boat and find your seat. The journey from Bodrum to Rhodes is approximately 2 hours and 20 minutes. During this time, you can enjoy incredible views over the Aegean Sea and other Greek islands.

Rhodes Island:

Rhodes is the largest and most famous island in the complex of Dodecanese. With its long history and deep-rooted culture, the island attracts millions of visitors every year who wish to spend their holidays there. Once you arrive there, you will be given some hours of free time to explore the island according to your interests.

History:

Rhodes has a plethora of museums and places of interest to visit to explore and learn its long history. The island is also known as the Island of the Knights due to its interesting medieval history. In the old medieval city of Rhodes, you can walk inside a fortified city and observe interesting monuments, such as the medieval castle and other museums.

Beaches:

Those who are interested to spend their time in a more laid-back way, the island has exceptional beaches. You can find many beaches to relax and enjoy swimming close to the city center. There are also beach bars with a vibrant atmosphere, upbeat music, and refreshing cocktails in case you wish to upgrade your experience.

Culinary Tradition:

Since there are no meals included in this excursion, participants can enjoy a delicious meal in one of the many restaurants that Rhodes has. More precisely, there are traditional taverns and restaurants serving freshly made Greek and local recipes. Thus, do not miss the chance to taste some really tasty food options and drinks some local signature drinks.

Return:

On a pre-arranged time, you will return back to the port from where the boat departs. The return ferry trip will be approximately 2 hours and 20 minutes. When you arrive back to Bodrum, you will be full of positive feelings, long-lasting memories, and great images.
