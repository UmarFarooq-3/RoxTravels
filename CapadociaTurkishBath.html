Capadocia Turkish Bath

Discover the Tranquility of Turkish Hammam in Cappadocia with ROX Travels

Are you ready to immerse yourself in the age-old tradition of Turkish hammam and experience the epitome of relaxation in the enchanting landscapes of Cappadocia? Join ROX Travels on a journey to rejuvenate both body and soul in a unique and authentic Turkish spa experience.

Turkish Hammam Experience: All-In-One Bliss

At ROX Travels, we offer an all-inclusive Turkish hammam experience that goes beyond the traditional bath. Our package encompasses the spa, massage, and even a refreshing swimming pool to ensure you indulge in the ultimate relaxation.

What's Included in the Turkish Hammam Experience?
  • Spa Delights: Begin your journey with a session in the Turkish hammam, a ritual that dates back centuries. The steamy atmosphere opens your pores, preparing your skin for the next stages of pampering.
  • Massage Magic: Feel the stress melt away as skilled therapists work their magic with a rejuvenating massage. Whether you prefer a gentle touch or a more invigorating massage, our professionals tailor the experience to your preferences.
  • Swimming Pool Serenity: After the spa and massage, take a dip in our tranquil swimming pool. Let the cool waters soothe your senses and provide the perfect finale to your Turkish hammam experience.

ROX Travels Services: Unparalleled Convenience

  • Inclusions:
    • Turkish Hammam Package: Spa session, massage, and access to the swimming pool.
    • Professional Masseurs: Highly trained therapists ensuring a top-notch experience.
    • Quality Facilities: Immerse yourself in a clean, comfortable, and authentic Turkish hammam environment.
  • Exclusions:
    • Additional Spa Treatments: Any extra services beyond the standard Turkish hammam package.
    • Personal Expenses: Souvenirs, snacks, or any other personal expenses during the excursion.
Duration:

The entire Turkish hammam experience with ROX Travels typically lasts around 2 to 3 hours, providing ample time to savor each stage of the rejuvenating journey.

Cancellation and Postponement Policies:

  • Cancellation:
    • Free cancellation 48 hours before the scheduled ride
    • 50% refund for cancellations made within 24-48 hours.
    • No refund for cancellations within 24 hours.
  • Postponement:
    • Guests can postpone the Turkish hammam up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any additional charges
