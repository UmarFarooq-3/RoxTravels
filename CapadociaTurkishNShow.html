CAPPADOCIA TURKISH NIGHT SHOW

About Us

Discover the magic of Cappadocia through an enchanting Turkish Night organized by ROX Travels. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry of Turkey while enjoying a night filled with traditional entertainment, delicious cuisine, and vibrant performances.

Turkish Night Details:

1. Starting Time and Duration:
  • in winter: 7:30 to 10:30
  • in summer: 8:30 to 10:30
    • 2. Pick-up and Drop-off:

    ROX Travels takes care of your transportation needs, with pick-up from your accommodation and a comfortable drop-off after the event. Our professional drivers ensure a safe and pleasant journey, allowing you to focus on the upcoming cultural extravaganza.

    3. Dinner Options:

    Choose between delectable vegetarian and non-vegetarian dinner options, prepared with local flavors and culinary expertise. Our chefs ensure a delightful dining experience, catering to diverse palates and dietary preferences.

    4. Beverage Choices:

    Enjoy the night with unlimited options of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, carefully selected to complement your meal and enhance the overall experience.

    5. Turkish Night Dances:

    Immerse yourself in the rhythmic beats and mesmerizing moves of various Turkish dances. Experience the artistry of traditional dances like the captivating belly dance, the energetic folk dances, and more.

    6. Audience Participation:

    .For those who want to do more than just watch, our Turkish Night encourages audience participation. Feel the rhythm, join the dance, and become a part of the vibrant celebration of Turkish culture

    Cancellation and Postponement Policy:

    • ROX Travels understands that plans can change. Cancellations made 48 hours before the event receive a full refund.
    • : In case of unexpected changes, guests can opt to postpone their Turkish Night experience to a later date with no additional charges.

    Age Limit:

    The Turkish Night is a family-friendly event, suitable for all ages. There is no specific age limit, and we encourage families and individuals of all ages to join us for this cultural extravaganza. However, please note that alcoholic beverages will only be served to guests above the legal drinking age.
