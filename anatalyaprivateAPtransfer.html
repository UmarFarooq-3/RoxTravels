Private Antalya Airport Transfer

The Private Antalya Airport Transfer is the most convenient, fast, and economical way to reach your hotel in Antalya. No waiting for other people or big buses to be fully loaded. Our driver will be waiting for you at the airport at your arrival time. The vehicles used for private transfers are clean and comfortable. Drivers do not make any stops without your permission on the way to your hotel; they will take you straight to your destination. The cost of this service is much lower than a taxi from the Airport to Antalya. We use Mercedes Vito Minibuses (or similar) with a seating capacity of 6 people.

You can book our private airport transfer service to any hotel in Antalya.

Don’t Forget
  • We charge per vehicle, not per person. When you come in a group, you are assigned a vehicle for your entire party.
  • We provide transfers across a whole range of hotels in and around Antalya. Avail our most comfortable and affordable private transfer service from Antalya airport and ensure a hassle-free holiday in Antalya.
  • Drivers will wait no longer than 5 minutes after the scheduled pickup time.
  • Our operation team will give you the exact time of pick-up a day before the tour date.
  • Please wait at the outside of the main security gate of your hotel 10 minutes before your scheduled pickup time.
Hotel Pick-up Note:

For small hotels in Antalya's old town region (KALEICI HOTELS), buses can't enter the narrow streets. Please meet us in front of McDonald’s. More details will be provided.
