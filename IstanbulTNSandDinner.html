Istanbul Turkish Night Show & Dinner

Get away from the crowded atmosphere of Istanbul by visiting the beautiful Princess Islands. The complex includes nine beautiful Islands that have a unique charm and a great atmosphere. This full day excursion takes you to visit two of the largest and most popular. Refresh your senses, send the stress away, admire the beauty of nature, and the serenity of the islands, while making new memories with your friends or family.

During your holidays in Istanbul, you can join an Istanbul Turkish Night Show and experience first-hand the main cultural aspects of the country. This show is ideal for those who wish to add an extra touch of fun to their holidays. The show immerses participants into an unforgettable cultural exploration with folklore music, colorful costumes, traditional dances, and authentic local recipes.

Istanbul Turkish Night Show takes place on a daily basis and has an approximate duration of 4 hours. The activity starts during the evening. A comfortable car will pick you up from your hotel in the city center and drive you towards the restaurant where the show is hosted. When you arrive at the restaurant you will be welcomed and sit at one of the premium tables. The facility maintains a traditional and authentic character in order to enhance your experience.

Pick-up time: 19:00PM

Pick Up: The show begins during the evening and lasts for four hours. A modern vehicle will pick you up from your hotel at a pre-arranged time. Then, you will arrive at a local restaurant where the show takes place. The authentic atmosphere will be one of the first things that you will notice upon entrance.

Dinner:

Participants of this activity enjoy the show from one of the premium tables that the restaurant has. When you are seated, a delicious Turkish dinner will be offered to you. The meal includes various options able to satisfy your individual taste. Throughout the duration of the show, you will be able to enjoy unlimited consumption of soft drinks. It must be noted that alcoholic beverages are served at an extra cost.

Show:

After dinner, the show starts, so get ready to enjoy the most of this spectacular experience. The show includes traditional folklore dancing and music. During this phase, you will be able to observe the colorful folklore costumes of the dancers. The traditional music will create an enhancing atmosphere and present you the cultural diversities that coexist in Turkey. In addition, you will listen to classic Turkish music and be able to identify the main traditional music instruments.

Music and Dance:

Then, you will watch an amazing Asok Masuk show. This show is an imitation play of dwarfs. It was originally started in the Taseli district of Silifke and is usually performed by 2 male dancers. What follows is a belly dance show during which you will be able to dance and learn the basic moves. The night continues with Turkish music played by a DJ and lots of dancing from you.

Return:

Turkish Night Show lasts for 4 hours. Thus, by the end of the show, a comfortable car will be waiting for you. This amazing experience finishes when you arrive back at your hotel in the city center.
