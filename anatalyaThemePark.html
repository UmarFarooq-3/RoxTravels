During your holidays in Antalya, enjoy a full day excursion that combines excitement and laughter all in one! Visit the Land of Legends Theme Park, known as the “Turkish Disneyland,” offering amusement park and waterpark experiences, along with various activities like a 5D cinema and shows. Make your holidays unforgettable by spending a day in this park.

Pick Up:

The Antalya Land of Legends Tour starts during the morning. Our team members will pick you up from your hotel and drive you towards the Land of Legends Theme Park, which is located at the area of Belek.

Coasters in the Theme Park:

The park will capture your attention since the very first moments due to its size, interior design and the variety of activities that it includes. It is clearly not a coincidence that the park is among the biggest in Europe of its kind. Its exterior and interior design are characterized by a common theme with several structures, sculptures and specially designed green areas full of trees and flowers. Land of Legends features an impressive amusement park ideal for every visitor. Even for those who wish to experience some adrenaline boosts, the amusement park includes a variety of roller coasters. Among them there is one particular roller coaster that is famous. The Typhoon is 43m high coaster that runs with an 86Kh/h speed. The interesting highlight of this activity is of course the grand splashy finishing.

More Fun:

Among others, visitors can also enjoy an amazing ride with the Hyper Coaster. This particular activity combines excitement with high speed, ensuring that you enjoy this experience at its maximum. Despite the roller coasters, the park includes a variety of other activities where you can spend your time depending on your own personal interests. These activities include among others, Family Coaster, Sky Fighter, Air Balloon Race, Flying Carpet, Twister, Family Swing, Hurricane, Uptown Loop, Finger Coaster, Galleon, Race Coaster, Sweet Swing, Sky Walker, Round the World, Riot, Tagawa, Carousel and Power Fall.

Slides in the Theme Park:

Within the Land of Legends Theme Park visitors can also find an impressive waterpark. It includes 40 slides in total, multiple pools and specially designed shallow pools for the kids. The slides are suitable for all ages and for all the interests, as you can choose between relaxed and slow slides or speedy and electrifying ones. In what regards the pools there are multiple types to choose according to your mood. For example, the Wave Shock looks like a small ocean where you can enjoy riding the waves and relax. For relaxing and playful moments with your family and friends, you can also enjoy the Family Floats pool. Children can enjoy playing and swimming in the Aqua Tower. In what regards the more exciting side of the pools, those who wish to have fun and adventure have also some alternatives. The Rafting Rapids pools allow you to ride foamy and white waters, while the Wave Ball is a pool that makes calm waters into wild ones in a short period of time.

Aqua Disco:

An exciting feature of this waterpark is the Aqua Disco where you can enjoy dancing fountains with music and lighting features that produce an exclusive and exceptional result. Some other waterpark’s activities are, among others, the Infinity Pool, Lazy Floats, Baby Pool, Wild River, Activity Pool, Spray Action, My Dive, Twister Racer, Tunnel Deep Dive, Turbulence, Speedway, Rainbow, Upside Down, Sea Voyager, Tower falls, Windstream, Space Rocket, Manicone, Abyss, Challenger, Starship, Happy Waters and Deep Dive.

Interesting Activities:

Land of Legends Theme Park includes a number of other interesting activities. One among the interesting highlights of the park is the 5D cinema. The particular room that hosts this activity has moving seats and the room makes use of some really fascinating special effects. During your time in the cinema room, you may feel the wind, the rain, the fog, lasers and lightings. These effects are able to transform your experience into a truly memorable one by providing a spectacular cinematic adventure.

Dolphin Show:

Antalya Land of Legends Park includes a variety of spectacular shows and activities that are ideal for all ages. Within the park, visitors can enjoy an amazing dolphin show. During the dolphin show you will be able to observe and admire these beautiful sea creatures. In addition, you will be able to understand how intelligent and friendly they are. For those who are interested, there are additional dolphin activities. For example, you can enjoy a 30-minute swimming session with dolphins or just take photos with them, at an extra cost.

Beluga Whales Show:

The shows include also an outstanding Beluga Whales show. These sea creatures are ranked among the biggest sea animals; thus, your experience will be unforgettable. For those who are interested, the park offers extra activities where you can take photos with the infamous beluga whales, at an extra cost. Another attraction is of course the lovely penguins that the park hosts. You will be able to observe their behavior and their house. These cute animals are the official far-away guest starts of the park.

Return Back:

The park closes its doors during the afternoon. After having spent your day there, a vehicle will be waiting to transfer your back. This exciting excursion ends by the time you arrive at your hotel in Antalya.
